An introduction to probability and statistics concepts frequently used in engineering applications. Topics include probability theory, random variables, distributions, data collection, sampling, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing, ANOVA, and regression analysis, all with relevant industry applications.

**Prerequisites:** None (fundamental algebra, calculus, and set theory expected)
