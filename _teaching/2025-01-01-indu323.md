An introduction to deterministic mathematical models with emphasis on linear programming. Applications to production, logistics, and service systems. Computer solution of optimization problems.

**Prerequisites:** ENGR 213; ENGR 233; INDU 211

## Topics

| Topic | Textbook (Hillier & Lieberman, 11th Ed.) |
|---|---|
| Introduction to operations research | Ch. 1–2 |
| Introduction to linear programming | Ch. 3 |
| The simplex method | Ch. 4–5 |
| Duality theory | Ch. 6 |
| Sensitivity analysis | Ch. 7 |
| The dual simplex method | Ch. 8 |
| The transportation and assignment problems | Ch. 9 |
