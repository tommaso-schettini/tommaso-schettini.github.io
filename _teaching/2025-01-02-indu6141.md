A comprehensive study of mathematical models and techniques for logistics management. Covers operational, tactical, and strategic planning problems including supply chain characterization, site location, distribution planning, vehicle routing, fleet and crew scheduling, demand management, replenishment management, and revenue management.

**Prerequisites:** INDU 324 (Operations Research II); knowledge of Python

## Topics

| Topic | Textbook (Ghiani et al., 3rd Ed.) |
|---|---|
| Introduction to logistics systems | Ch. 1 |
| Forecasting logistics data | Ch. 2 |
| Design of logistics networks | Ch. 3 |
| Selection of suppliers | Ch. 4 |
| Managing a warehouse | Ch. 5 |
| Freight transportation planning | Ch. 6 |
| Project presentations | — |
